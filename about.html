Acadia INC.
A culture of thinkers & makers.

Acadia was founded in 1997 as a design and technology company. 17 years later the landscape has changed dramatically—our industry has evolved, our clients’ businesses and their challenges have become more complex, consumer behavior has changed—and we have changed. Yet, 17 years ago Acadia was started with the belief that intuitive and engaging consumer experiences will help move our clients’ businesses forward—and we still believe that today.

    At our core, we are a culture of thinkers and makers. We aren’t a consultancy whose end deliverable is merely a PowerPoint presentation. We aren’t just a production company that executes someone else’s vision. We develop insightful strategies rooted in consumer behavior and the digital space. We turn those insights and strategies into experiences consumers ultimately engage and interact with.

    The experiences we create are multi-platform, both on and offline, and, when working together, create the ultimate reflection of our client’s brand.

We create new ways to get inspired.

We invent. We tinker. We experiment with new technologies. We share snippets of our life experiences with each other. It helps influence the way we think and the way we work.

Research & Development

We foster a culture of innovation and experimentation and spend a lot of time and energy on our internal Research & Development practices. Our developers rely on R & D to stretch their imaginations and create things to move our clients’ businesses forward.

Small Talks

Once a month, a small group of people from across different departments give a quick talk about something (anything) they want to share. Some recent topics have included a pilgrimage to India, productivity hacking and What’s in a Name?

Department Outings

Every quarter, each department goes on an outing together to bond over a new exhibit, a lecture by a coveted speaker or even just over some drinks. The goal is to explore something the city has to offer, often trying something completely new together.

Our Departments
Project & Program Management
We’ve built our client relations model through the utilization of Producers & Program Managers.
Our Producers work directly with our clients and, at the same time, oversee the production teams running the projects. They’re the liaison between our clients’ needs and our team’s creative solutions. Our producers are the cornerstone to our process, an efficiency lost on many agencies where these separate disciplines often convene in passing. Program Managers are rooted in production, understand the digital landscape and the process for making great work happen. Our Program Managers and Executive Producers oversee and manage larger client relationships and work closely with Producers on every project.
Strategy & Analytics
The Strategy & Analytics team at Acadia is comprised of creative problem solvers.
Our strategists and analytics leads not only understand brands, but also how brands can most effectively connect with consumers through their systems of experiences—both online and off. The team employs an agile model to strategy development to tackle our clients’ challenges, knowing that each is distinct and requires a similarly unique approach. The team also specializes in developing behavioral insights that inspire work that effectively and measurably connects with target consumers on both functional and emotional levels.
Creative & UX
Our Creative Department is made up of best-in-class designers, copywriters and user experience designers.
This team is involved in many stages of the process from the initial concepting to the production of the product and even through development and QA. They work closely with the Strategy team in the initial discovery phases of a project and also with our Technology & Development teams to ensure the final experience is one that our client and their consumers will be excited about. Some additional talents in the creative department include magic, skateboarding, sewing and illustration.
Technology & Development
Acadia fosters a culture of innovation—allowing ideas to become more powerful with every brainstorm.
Our team of technologists and developers offer a wide range of skills including front and backend development, website, mobile and tablet development, interactive installations, ecommerce and custom content management tools. Our tech team is comprised of tireless inventors who spend a lot of time and energy on our internal Research & Development practices. Our developers rely on R&D to stretch their imaginations. Then, working closely with our designers, they create things that have never been seen before.
