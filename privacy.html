Acadia Inc. Privacy Policy
Last modified: April 30, 2014 (download)
The following are the Acadia Technology Inc. Privacy policies for its web site (www.acadiatechnologycom). We do update our policy periodically without giving any notice so please check occasionally for any changes.
Acadia Technology take privacy very seriously and these data collected from our registered member will be used only for providing them service as well as for their benefits. This privacy policy discloses the practices for Acadia Technology to protect privacy for users of its “Toolbar” beta users. From herein, the "Service" is defined as the user support services owned and operated by Acadia Technology and the “Acadia Technology Toolbar” or “Toolbar” is defined as the desktop toolbar supplied by Acadia Technology to identified users during the Beta Testing phase. Submitting your information on our website or through the toolbar constitutes acceptance of this privacy Policy.
1. Information Collection & Use:
Acadia Technology is the sole owner of the information collected through the web site/phone/e-mail. We will not sell or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this policy. Acadia Technology collects information from the users at several different points through our toolbar.
A). Personal Information we will ask you when we need information that personally identifies you (personal information) or allows us to contact you. In order to use the Acadia Technology Service and the toolbar user must first complete the registration form. During registration user is required to give user contact information (such as First and Last name, E-mail address, Postal address, Personal identifier etc.). Acadia Technology will use your contact details to contact you regarding the product and/or services you have requested. This includes re-registration/renewal notifications, special offers, and surveys on improving our service.

B). Payment Information If user registers to use the Service provided by Acadia Technology, we will collect credit card information, and other payment information necessary to complete a payment from user. Acadia Technology may use a third party to process and verify credit cards for billing purposes. This requires us to share user name, credit card number, expiration date and billing address with the credit card processing company. When processing a payment transaction, Acadia Technology will also obtain information regarding the amount of the payment and other transaction data. We may transfer or disclose this payment information to a third party only to the extent necessary in order to complete the payment processing.
2. Computer System Information:
A). Subscriber Computer - We may also ask for information related to your computer. This may include: information about the date of purchase of your computer, type of computer, identification number of your computer, make and model of your computer and/or any computer hardware, software or peripherals attached to it, condition of the computer, system and registry data about software installations and hardware configurations, and error tracking files. Generally, this information is required to provide personalized technical support to you, and to help us update our support tools and enhance our supported products list.

B). Remote Access - While requesting supports you may request for the problem to be resolved by taking control of your computer. Acadia Technology uses qualified tools which allow user to grant control of user computer to an Expert remotely over the Internet, so that the Expert can diagnose or correct problems. Experts are not allowed to use the Remote Access software until and unless they have received adequate training in its use, and user have consented to grant control. In addition, Experts will not use Remote Access tools to obtain confidential or sensitive information stored on user computer or network, deliberately destroy information on user computer or network, or cause user to experience system problems.
At the time of placing an order, register and request a service, apply for free registration or engage in certain other activities on our website you may need to provide some information. For example: When you register online with us, you may ask to provide your name, email ID, phone number and address etc.
You may required to provide some information like your registered user ID and information about your computer hardware, software and the nature of the problem you are experiencing at the time of technical support by email or phone.
It is your decisions whether to precede with any activity that requests personal information. However, please note that if you do not wish to provide the requested information, you may not be able to complete the specific process.
3. Fair Usage Policy, Suspension or Termination of Subscription:
Though Acadia Technology has no limits on the amount of telephone or online support requests a Subscription based plan user may make during the subscription period, however, each Subscriber's use of the support services for the subscription based plans are subject to "fair use" policy. Under this policy, if at any time, in Acadia Technology sole discretion, a subscription based plan user is found to be abusing the service by exceeding the level of use reasonably expected from someone using a Subscription based Plan for individual use, and then Acadia Technology reserves the right to suspend or terminate Subscriber's Subscription Services. In addition, Acadia Technology reserves the right to suspend or terminate any Subscription Services of any Subscriber that Acadia Technology, in its sole discretion, determines are being used (a) fraudulently, (b) by any person other than Subscriber, or (c) for any computer system other than a Registered System. User may terminate the Service at any time by giving electronic notice to Acadia Technology provided, however, that User will not be entitled to a refund of any fees prepaid by User for the Service.
4. General License Restrictions:
Any other use of the Acadia Technology Portal, Services, Materials or Software, other than as explicitly permitted by Acadia Technology is prohibited. Rights to execute, copy, modify, display, transmit, distribute, manufacture, use, sale are all reserved to Acadia Technology and its suppliers. Reverse engineering and decompilation of the Software is strictly prohibited.
